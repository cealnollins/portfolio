My top 3 shows of all time

Vikings

Who could go wrong with vikings, there is tons of battle scenes great characters and some actual history sprinkled in. Overall its a great show and ive seen it many times.

The Mandalorian

In a galaxy far far away you can find both a kick ass and wholesome show for all ages. Starwars fans finally got a great show that made them forget about the sequals they have been complaining about for so long.

Chainsaw Man

I had to include atleast one anime in this list and it had to be chainsaw man. Chainsawman is such a good show with character develoment, amazing fight animations, and pure comedy gold.
